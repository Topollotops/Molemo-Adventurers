Services We Offer

We offer the following services.

  • Bungee Jumping

    • Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush as you leap from a towering platform, secured only by a bungee cord, and freefall towards the ground below before rebounding back up.

  • Zip-lining

    • Soar through the air on a suspended cable, taking in breath taking views and feeling the wind rush past you as you glide from one platform to another.

  • Extreme Camping

    • Test your survival skills and immerse yourself in the great outdoors with extreme camping, where you will rough it in remote and challenging environments, often with minimal amenities.

  • Hiking and Trekking

    • Explore stunning natural landscape on foot, traversing trails of varying difficulty levels, from leisurely strolls through scenic vistas to challenging multi-day treks in remote wilderness areas.

  • Mountaineering

    • Scale towering peaks, navigating challenging routes and experiencing breath taking vistas from the summit.

  • Mountain Biking

    • Blaze down rugged trails, navigating rocky terrain and steep descents for an exhailarating ride.

  • Ice Climbing

    • Ascend frozen waterfalls and glaciers,wielding ice axes and crampons for a truly challenging adventure.

  • Skiing and Snowboarding

    • Conquer steep slopes and deep powder in the winter months, enjoying the thrill of high-speed descents

  • Rock Climbing

    • Scale vertical rock faces using hands, feet, and equipment for a challenging physical and mental workout.
